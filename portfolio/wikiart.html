WikiArt

How might art be more accessible? How can we help people see, understand, and enjoy art?

Defining the crowd as working professionals, university students, and hobbyists, WikiArt was reimagined to respond to their needs as one of the most complete repositories of fine art online. The result is the ability to identify artwork, discover nearby shows, and pull together resources to form a complete story around a piece and artist.
